Site Name = First Choice Weather

Purpose of Site:

The purpose of this site is to provide weather updates for the people who live in or plan to travel to Preston, Soda Springs, or Fish Haven. It will provide storm updates and also have a gallary page of scenic pictures from these locations. This will help the population of these areas be better prepared for their day to day life. It will provide a quick source and accurate source to check the weather.

Domain Name: firstchoiceweather.org
