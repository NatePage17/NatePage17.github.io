Target Audience:

The target audience for this site is those living in or planning to travel to Preston, Soda Springs or Fish Haven. It will most likely be adults who have children and older individuals who check this site on a daily basis.

Two Personas

Persona 1: Your Average soccer mom: Everyday is a whole new adventure this mom. Getting all the kids up dressed, lunches packed, and in the car on the way to school is quite the task and achievement. This mom needs a reliable site to make sure she gets her kids ready for the elements that lie outside.

Persona 2: Active retired couple: These two are living life to the fullest. Now that they are retired each new day has something to offer. Whether that be Pickleball at the park with friends, or relaxing by the pool. These two have endless options to choose from. Knowing an accurate forcast is essential for a productive retired life. Having an accurate weekend forecast is a must have for this audience. If they miss out on a weekend getaway because of a false weather report, you can bet they will not be revisiting your site.

Scenarios

  • 1) Your kids are playing soccer in Preston this afternoon. It looks like it may rain, should you have your kids bring all their rain gear?
  • 2) It's late march in Fish Haven. You and your guy friends are contemplating taking the motorcycles out for the weekend. What are the possibilities of their being a storm?
  • 3) There was just a huge snow storm in Soda Springs, what are the current driving conditions looking like?
  • 4) Heat index is higher than it has ever been in Preston, city puts out an order to stay inside if at all possible.
  • 5) Your average person wants a reliable forecast he can check quickly each day.
